Biography


I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) , under the supervision of Prof. Bei Yu from Fall 2022. Previously, I received my B.S. in the Department of Software Engineering from Central South University, and my M.S. in the Department of Computer Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen).

Besides research, I love playing badminton in my spare time.
